Fire TV Stick 4K Max

Darren Twa April 14, 2023 Tech

Excellent 9/10

I purchased the Fire TV Stick 4K Max from Amazon.

Â 

Rational and Concerns

I do not connect my TV to the internet. It has mostly been connected to a computer and streaming content has been accessed through a web browser.

I first bought the Fire TV Stick to connect to my computer monitor. However I have an extra wide monitor and the Fire TV does not support that resolution and the aspect ratio get mangled. So that was disappointing.

So I connected it to my TV.

Use

The Fire TV connected to a television works really well. I went the extra mile and also paired it with my Echo Studio speakers. What a great combination.

Rating

The Fire TV Stick 4K Max is excellent, but I wish it supported more resolutions and aspect ratios. 9/10

Buy it on Amazon
