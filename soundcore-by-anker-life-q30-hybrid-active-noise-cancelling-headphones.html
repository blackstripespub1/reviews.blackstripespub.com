Soundcore by Anker Life Q30 Hybrid Active Noise Cancelling Headphones

Darren Twa April 14, 2023 Tech

Great 8/10

I purchased the Anker Soundcore Q30 headphones from Amazon.

Â 

Rational

I can't say that I really needed these, and I haven't used them very much. Mostly I wanted something that could easily be used with my Fire TV for occasions when the house is a bit too noisy or needs to be too quiet.

Concerns

I always have the same concerns about headphones: comfort and sound quality.

Use

Connecting these to other devices through bluetooth is very easy. I have used them with both my Fire TV and my Pixel 7 without any issues.

I do not like the fit very much. The top bar seems to "lean" forward and not sit squarely on the top of my head. For TV watching that is not really an issue. I have worn these vacuuming the house and they shift forward quite a bit and need attention.

As for sound quality I have no complaints. There is an easy "pass through" to quickly turn off the noise cancelling and I really hate it. Instead of being a button, it only requires a touch and it too easy to turn on.

Rating

Great. 8/10

Buy it on Amazon
