Blue Yeti USB Microphone

Darren Twa April 14, 2023 Tech

Excellent 9/10

I have purchased several of these from Amazon, but only one was a replacement for one that stopped working. Be sure to check various colors to get best price.

Rational

I needed a good quality microphone to do online video streaming. I had already bought one previously for my children to use and was happy enough with the quality to get another one for myself.

Use

This microphone works equally well as a USB device while using Linux or Windows. One feature I like is that I can plug my Sony MDR7506 headphones right into the microphone and use the volume control on the Blue Yeti.
