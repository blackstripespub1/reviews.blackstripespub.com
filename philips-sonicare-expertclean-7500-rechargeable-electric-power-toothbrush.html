Philips Sonicare ExpertClean 7500, Rechargeable Electric Power Toothbrush

Darren Twa April 14, 2023 Home

Outstanding 10/10

I purchased the Sonicare ExpertClean 7500 from Amazon.

 

Rational

My dental hygienist has be encouraging me to get both an electric toothbrush and a water flosser. I finally made the change and am very happy I did.

Concerns

I had an electric toothbrush from another brand (Oral-B) in the past and cannot say I was completely thrilled by it. That experience delayed my purchase of the Sonicare. What a difference between that past toothbrush and this one.

Use

Nothing but praise for this brush. I recommend reading the instructions so that you understand the angles that you should be using to brush your teeth. Other than that, let the brush do the work.

Rating

The Philips Sonicare ExpertClean 7500 exceeded my expectations. 10/10

Buy it on Amazon
